PORTFOLIO PROJECT

This project was developed as part of the final training course for Argentina Programa, using Angular, Spring Boot, MySQL

Main idea

It is a resume of my profile as developer. It imitates the LinkedIn profile view, but developed by me and fully customizable.

Philosophy

I believe people are as dynamic as the environment they are inmersed into. Roadmaps are guides but they are not written on stone, is with this spirit that I believe it is not important the time it took a person to finish a study but the lessons learned, the people met, and the footprints left. That's why, among other details, in the skills component I used an animated progress bar to reveal them as dynamic and trainable.
